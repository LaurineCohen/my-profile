Sky Diving

I am passionate about Extreme Sports. I wish to focus on my passion and thus find a way to work in the field of extreme sports management and, in parallel with my work, join a professional skydiving team. Being an active sportswoman has always motivated me to explore and keep up to date with sports news. What inspires me most is the way sport makes people happy and dreamy. Athletes are defined by passion and determination, and this very unique way to tell a story is what echoes to me the most.

Music

I am passionate about Music. Listen to it, but especially play it. Indeed, since I was a little girl I have been playing the piano. To be able to interpret the music I love is a joy. I sometimes give lessons and I also play in retirement homes. I think that music is an even more beautiful art when it is shared.

Travels

I am passionate about Travelling. Travelling makes everyone dream... There is nothing more beautiful in my opinion than spending your life discovering cultures and learning from others. To be able to live incredible experiences by creating memories with people from all over the world... To discover unusual landscapes... To open your mind to something else... At my young age, I was able to visit more than twenty countries. I am very lucky and very grateful to my parents for letting me go. I think this is where my open-mindedness and social skills come from, and it helps me every day.
